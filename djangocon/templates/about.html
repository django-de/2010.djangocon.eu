About

DjangoCon Europe 2010

DjangoCon Europe is a Django conference that aims to bring together the community and provide a wide range of sessions, panels, lightning talks and showcases of Django usage within various businesses. We aim to educate and bring people together to turn new ideas into working code!

The conference is organized by the German Django Association and is located in Berlin, Germany.
