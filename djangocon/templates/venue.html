Venue

Conference Hall - Palisa.de

The venue is an extensively restored industrial monument built in 1899 and is just a few minutes away from Alexanderplatz, a large square in Berlin's city center.

Lodging

Unfortunately, attempts to reserve a block of rooms at a hotel close to the venue were unsuccessful. The practical impact on attendees is that hotel reservations must be made individually.

Regardless, we hope that most djangonauts will be staying at the same hotels for practical and social reasons. To that end, a list of suitable hotels is being compiled and will be posted here, as well as announced via the conference blog.
