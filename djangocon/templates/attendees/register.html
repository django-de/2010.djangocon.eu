Additional information

Sutdent rate

The student rate is for full-time students only and limited in number. Academics and employees of non-profits should register at the regular rate.
To purchase a student ticket, contact us (feedback@djangocon.eu) to apply for a voucher to enable the ticket. Please don't forget to send a proof (timetable or valid student card) too.

Business rates

If your company is paying for you to attend DjangoCon, you should register at the business rate. Receipts for companies are only available at this rate.
By purchasing tickets at business rate, you'll be helping to sell more tickets at student rate.

Special notes on VAT-ID

European companies can fill in their VAT-ID and choose the VAT-free business ticket. We can only provide VAT-free tickets if the stated VAT-ID can be validated.
