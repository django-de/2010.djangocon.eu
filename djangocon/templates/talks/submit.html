Talk Submission Guidelines

We've done our best to keep the administrativa to a minimum, please read the guidelines carefully before submitting a talk.

Dates

  • April 15th: submission deadline
  • April 22nd: acceptance announcements

Speaker Registration

DjangoCon Europe is a volunteer-run conference set in the heart of old-world socialsm; everybody pays to attend (including speakers). Talk submission is not a substitute for purchasing a ticket. If you plan on giving a talk, please make sure to purchase conference tickets and arrange transportation/lodging for yourself.

Talks are Public

Talks will be recorded and published online following the conference; by submitting a talk, speakers implicitly authorize the conference organizers to record, edit, and publish audio and video of their presentation.

Format Guidelines

A standard talk is 45 minutes in length, composed of 30 minutes content and 15 minutes for audience question & answer.
